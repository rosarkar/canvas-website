Frequently Asked Questions

Addressing common concerns about Canvas Protocol

What if publishers don't want to annoy users?

Canvas CAPTCHAs transform verification into engaging brand mini-games that users actually enjoy. Publishers control frequency through smart targeting - once per unique IP, after viewing multiple pages, or randomized sampling. The free market naturally balances user experience with revenue. If verification frequency impacts traffic, publishers adjust accordingly. We provide the infrastructure; market dynamics create the equilibrium.

How do you prevent CAPTCHA fatigue?

Market forces inherently prevent overuse. Publishers who implement excessive CAPTCHAs see traffic decline, directly impacting revenue. This creates natural pressure to optimize frequency. Additionally, our varied game mechanics and rotating brand experiences keep interactions fresh. Publishers can A/B test different frequencies to find their optimal balance between monetization and user retention.

How do you ensure operator reliability?

EigenLayer's cryptoeconomic security model incentivizes honest behavior through staking requirements and slashing mechanisms. Operators risk significant capital if they provide false attestations. Our redistributable slashing implementation means any collusion attempts result in immediate financial losses that compensate affected parties. The decentralized operator set and random selection further minimize coordination risks.

How do you handle regulatory compliance?

Canvas Protocol implements industry-standard KYC/AML procedures for advertisers and publishers, ensuring full regulatory compliance. We're building a legitimate business infrastructure that respects jurisdictional requirements. Our approach prioritizes seamless onboarding while maintaining necessary compliance checks. We're studying best practices from leading Web3 platforms to create a frictionless yet compliant user experience.

Why blockchain instead of traditional infrastructure?

Blockchain provides transparent, verifiable proof of human interaction that traditional systems cannot match. Every CAPTCHA completion creates an immutable on-chain record, eliminating ad fraud and ensuring advertisers only pay for genuine engagement. Smart contracts automate payments, removing intermediaries and reducing costs. The decentralized architecture prevents any single entity from controlling or manipulating the verification process.

What's your go-to-market strategy?

We're targeting mid-tier publishers (10K-100K daily visitors) who struggle with traditional ad revenue but maintain quality content. These sites need alternative monetization without compromising user experience. Initial focus includes tech blogs, gaming sites, and educational platforms where users expect some friction for free content. Our SDK requires minimal integration - just a few lines of code - making adoption straightforward.
